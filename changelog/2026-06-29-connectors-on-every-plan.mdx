**Shipped 2026-06-29 · Integrations**

Connectors are no longer a paid-only feature. Every plan — **Starter**,
**Individual**, and **Team** — can now connect external tools so Bird can search
them and save results into your knowledge base.

## What changed

- **Starter** now includes up to **3 connectors** per person.
- **Individual** raises that to **10** per person.
- **Team** stays **unlimited**.

Everything else about connectors is unchanged: it's read-only at the source,
authorisation runs through the provider's standard OAuth flow, and content
enters your knowledge base only when you ask Bird to save it. Restricted-mode
workspaces still can't use connectors yet.
