**Shipped 2026-05-21 · Capture**

The Capture Stream is now live. One workspace-wide chronological feed of every capture — voice notes, photos, OCR'd images, live recordings, AI-written summaries, file uploads — in the order they happened. Open it from the **Capture Stream** entry in the sidebar.

## What's in it

Every capture surfaces with its source-of-origin labelled clearly:

- A voice memo shows the **VOICE** label and a microphone icon — and an inline audio player you can hit play on without leaving the page.
- A note that was *transcribed from* a voice recording shows a **NOTE** label with a "From audio" pill on the right, and the original audio is one click away — the same inline player appears beneath the transcript.
- Images render as thumbnails inline; click to open full-size.
- Videos show a poster frame with a play overlay; click for inline playback.
- An AI-written summary carries the sparkle icon and an "AI written" pill so you always know what came from a model versus what came from you.

## Filterable, shareable

Four filter chips above the feed — **Source**, **Collection**, **Person**, **Time** — all multi-select, all round-tripped through the URL. Send a teammate a link to *"Mish's voice captures in the Pancreatic shield collection, last 7 days"* and they land on exactly that view.

## Hover for exact time

Every relative timestamp ("3 hours ago", "yesterday") shows the full local date and time on hover — including timezone — so you don't have to do mental arithmetic when reconstructing a session.

## Try it

Open any workspace, click **Capture Stream** in the left sidebar. Or go directly to `/capture-stream`.
