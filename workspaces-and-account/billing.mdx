## Who can manage billing

Only **Owners** and **Admins** can view or change anything in the Billing tab.
Members and Guests don't see the tab at all — they can use the workspace
normally, but they can't see the plan, change it, update the payment method,
add seats, or cancel.

If you're a Member who needs a payment method updated or a seat added, ask an
Owner or Admin in your workspace. Owners can be transferred via the Members
tab if the current owner has left.

## View your current plan

Go to **Settings > Billing**. You'll see your current plan, billing cycle, next
billing date, and usage bars for storage, members, and owned workspaces. Click
**Compare all features** above the plan cards to see the full matrix.

## Add or remove Team seats

On the Team plan, every paid member (Owner, Admin, or Member) counts as a seat.
Guests are free and never count toward seat usage — Team includes 4 guests
per paid seat. See [Invite team members](/workspaces-and-account/invite-team-members#guest-caps-per-plan) for the guest caps on every plan.

### Adding a seat

Open **Settings > Members** and invite a teammate as Owner, Admin, or Member.
If the invite would take you above your current seat count, Bower shows a
preview of the prorated charge for the remainder of the current billing period
before anything is charged. Confirm to proceed — the seat is added to your
subscription and Stripe issues a prorated invoice immediately. The new seat is
held for the invitee until they accept.

Bower does not silently top up your subscription. A seat is only added when an
admin reviews the preview and confirms the charge.

<Note>
If your workspace is still in the 14-day free trial and you haven't added a
payment method yet, the invite flow will ask you to add a card before the seat
can be purchased.
</Note>

### Revoking a pending invite

If you revoke a paid invitation before it's accepted, the seat is released
straight away and Stripe issues a prorated credit for the unused portion of the
current period. The credit is applied to your next invoice automatically.

### Removing an existing member

Removing a paid member from **Settings > Members** drops your seat count for
the next billing cycle. The current period is not prorated — you've already
paid for it, and the member retains no access after removal. Your next monthly
or annual invoice reflects the lower seat count.

## Manage your subscription

Click **Manage Billing** in the Billing tab to open the Stripe Customer Portal.
From there you can:

- Switch between Individual and Team
- Switch between monthly and annual billing
- Update your payment method
- View past invoices and receipts

Plan switches show the exact prorated charge in Stripe before you confirm.
Upgrades and cycle changes apply immediately; the new rate is invoiced on the
spot with credit for the time you've already paid for on the previous plan.

## Monthly vs annual billing

Both monthly and annual subscriptions renew automatically. A monthly plan
charges on the same day each month; an annual plan charges once a year on the
anniversary of the original purchase. Annual saves up to 20% per month versus
monthly across Individual and Team.

To switch cycles, open **Manage Billing** and choose the new cycle on the same
plan. The switch is prorated through Stripe — you'll see the exact charge or
credit before confirming.

## What happens if payment fails

If a payment fails, your workspace enters a **past due** state. You'll see a
warning banner in the Billing tab asking you to update your payment method.
Stripe automatically retries failed payments several times over the following
weeks. If all retries fail, your workspace is downgraded to the Starter plan.

While in past-due state, your existing data is preserved — nothing is deleted.
Features gated behind your paid plan may become restricted until payment is
resolved.

## Cancellation

To cancel, go to **Settings > Billing** and click **Downgrade to Free**. You
don't need to open the Stripe portal. After confirmation:

- You keep access to your paid plan until the end of the current billing
  period — monthly cancellations run out at the end of the month, annual
  cancellations at the end of the year
- The Billing tab shows a banner with your end date
- No further charges are made; Bower does not refund the unused portion of the
  current period
- At period end, your workspace moves to the Starter plan
- Your data is preserved — nothing is deleted
- Storage, member, workspace, and audit-retention limits revert to Starter
  levels
- Public share links will no longer be accessible

You can reactivate at any time before the end date by clicking **Reactivate
plan** in the same banner. Reactivation is instant, doesn't trigger a new
charge, and leaves your existing billing cycle untouched.

## Student discount

Students and researchers at accredited institutions can request a free Individual
plan. Click **Free for students** under the Individual card on the Billing tab to get
the contact details — we review requests manually.

## Workspace deletion and billing

If you delete a workspace that has an active subscription, the subscription is
cancelled automatically. No further charges will occur.
